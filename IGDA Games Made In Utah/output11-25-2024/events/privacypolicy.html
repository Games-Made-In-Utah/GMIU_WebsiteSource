Privacy Policy

Your privacy is important to us. This privacy statement explains the personal data Games Made In Utah processes, how we process it, and for what purposes.

1. Information We Collect

We collect personal information that you provide to us directly, such as when you sign up for our newsletter or when you contact us directly through the website. The data we collect may include your name, email address, and any other information you choose to provide.

2. How We Use Your Information

The information we collect is used to improve our website, provide newsletters or other services that users may opt into, and communicate with you about our offerings and updates.

3. Sharing Information

We do not share your personal data with third parties, except when required by law or necessary to provide services you've requested.

4. Security

We take reasonable precautions to ensure the security of your personal information. However, no method of transmission over the Internet is completely secure.

5. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this privacy policy from time to time. Any changes will be reflected on this page.

6. Contact Us

If you have any questions about this Privacy Policy, please contact us.
